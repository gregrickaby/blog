In the winter of 2018, I partnered with [Lisa Sabin-Wilson](https://lisasabin-wilson.com/) to tech edit the 4th edition of [_WordPress All-in-One for Dummies_](https://amzn.to/37MMDLp). This edition spotlights all things WordPress and is really eight books in one!

Here's a summary of all eight books:

1. WordPress Basics
2. Setting Up WordPress
3. Exploring the Dashboard
4. Examining SEO
5. Utilizing Plugins
6. Customizing Your Site
7. Publishing Your Site
8. Running Multiple Sites with WordPress

It can be purchased [wherever books are sold](https://amzn.to/37MMDLp).
